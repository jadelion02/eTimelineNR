聖經，中國和世界歷史時間表 - 使用說明

eTimeline (電子時間線) 是由三個時間線週期組成： 1. eTimeline of Creation 創造-遠離的過去(從創造天地到創造亞當和夏娃), 2. eTimeline of History 歷史-近期的過去(從創造亞當和夏娃到現在), 及 3. eTimeline of Revelaion 啟示-未來(從現在到新天新地)：

"遠離的過去"是從聖經記載的創造天地到創造亞當、夏娃，及從科學界描述的宇宙大爆炸到人類出現的時期。 "近期的過去"包括從聖經中的亞當到使徒保羅及著名人物和事件，以及從三皇五帝至蔡英文、習近平和從夏朝到中華民國，中華人民共和國，又從大禹治水到香港和澳門回歸中國。 "未來"是從現在到啟示錄記載的新天新地。

要使用時間線，請打開瀏覽器，複製並粘貼下面的鏈接並將其放置在 URL 空間中，然後按 Enter 鍵。可接到 eTimeline (https://jadelion02.github.io/eTimeline).

開啟屏幕

首次開啟網頁時，你會看到與下圖類似的圖像。 在此頁的左上方會有透明背景的選項。 可以通過點擊此選項區域之外的屏幕任何其他位置可關閉選項，或者點擊位於選項右上角的 X。 點擊屏幕左側下方的 可用來開啟選項。

此選項是本網站內所有網頁的頂層。 可用的選項由每個選項旁邊的三角形指示，你可以通過點擊 ▼ 向下箭頭將其擴張， 或點擊 ▲ 向上箭頭來縮短選項。

使用電子時間線

要使用電子時間線，請選擇選項中的"時間線"又進行選擇三個時間線其中之一的時間線。你可以選擇在圖片中心三個圓圈上方的時間軸名稱點擊來鏈接。如果你需要旋轉到不同的時間線，請點擊圖片中的圓圈之一或點擊圖片中向左或向右的箭頭。第三種選擇時間線的方法是選擇時間線標題名稱中的鏈接。除了選項，你可以切換語言為英文 (English ♥)，也能返回中文 eTimeline 時間線。

瀏覽

導航面板可以點擊靠近屏幕頂部按鈕旁邊的 （^ 符號）按鈕來開啟。導航面板具有瀏覽所需的大部分的控制按鈕。 導航面板上的控制按鈕（如右圖所示）控制時間線的向左或向右移動，其中四個按鈕在時間線頁面的右上角重複出現。如果你有滾輪鼠標，可以用滾動鼠標滾輪來控制向左或向右的移動，或者可用滾動鼠標滾輪光標在兩個時間刻度之間在屏幕中拖動來控制向左或向右。

"+"(加號)按鈕放寬電子時間線，而"-"(減號)按鈕用來縮窄電子時間線。這些縮放按鈕可以讓你用來作放寬或縮窄電子時間線。如果你發現搜索結果項目感覺得太狹窄，你可以按導航面板上的"+"(加號)或使用鍵盤"Ctrl"鍵和鼠標滾輪向上滾動功能來放寬展開時間線。但如果你希望在屏幕上看到更多項目，可以按"-"(減號)或使用鍵盤"Ctrl"鍵和鼠標滾輪向下滾動功能來壓縮時間線。偶爾，當搜索結果擠得太緊而屏幕移動出現凍結時，你可以刷新屏幕以恢復操作。

搜索

如要在數據庫中搜索人物或事件，請點擊導航面板的"找尋 ... ▾"框中按 ▼ 向下箭頭。 每個數據庫中的人物/角色或事件會有相關聯的編號 ID 。 拉下列表出現時，在找尋框下方的空白處輸入搜索項目。自動填寫的功能將在你鍵入時縮短搜索列表。當你找到項目時可以進行選擇，然後按"提交"按鈕 。搜索結果將會移至適當的時間範圍，並以粗體和與相鄰項目不同的顏色顯示。它通常會出現在屏幕中央附近，但如果你沒有看到該項目，你可能需要使用垂直滑塊向上或向下移動以定位顯示結果項目。

在導航面板的左側，你會看到在"找尋... ▾"框旁邊一個帶有“1”的白色矩形框（即），這是此網頁的開啟搜索的編號 ID 。每個搜索結果都會有一個與你的搜索操作相配的 ID 。如果你知道或記得人物或事件的 ID 編號，請輸入編號 ID 在框中，並按"提交"按鈕或放大鏡(即 )它便會跳到那個時間點。例如，在歷史電子時間線，ID 1.01 將帶你到 "諾亞"，1.02 將帶你到 "亞伯拉罕"。"提交"按鈕旁邊的複選框是用來作啟用/停用"提交"按鈕之間切換。

隱藏和顯現

每時間線分為橫向組，它夾在兩個時間上下時刻度之間。 每組與位於屏幕左側的標題相關，它可以通過按"隱藏"按鈕使每個組隱藏 。 隱藏組並不是刪除該組，而只是使其暫時隱藏，以便於閱讀其他項目。 點擊導航面板中的通用"顯現"按鈕 會使所有組再次顯現。

搜索 Bible Gateway (聖經網關) 和 Blue Letter Bible (藍字聖經)

"聖經網關"和"藍字聖經"網站是僅為方便讀者而設，這些網站是外方網站，與 eTimeline 沒有任何關係。如要使用這些網站，請打開電子時間線導航面板，點擊左下角附近導航面板的"+"(加號)。下拉板將顯示其各自網站的輸入框。只需在輸入框中輸入你的搜索主題，然後按"提交"按鈕。

雜項

eTimeline of History "歷史-電子時間線"是基於從"1"開始的希伯來曆法(即零年，1 相當於希伯來拉比傳統的公元前3,761年)，但如果你將鼠標懸停在興趣點上時，公曆的年份便會出現。列在帶問號的綠色小方塊下方(即)會有項目的解釋。只需將鼠標光標懸停在它上面，就會顯示一個下拉選項。如果你有觸摸屏，你可以用手指控制放大和縮小(捏合縮放)屏幕(即相當於屏幕頂部的 + 和 - 符號)。

其他的時間線，eTimeline of Creation "創世-電子時間線"是基於聖經創世記中的時間尺度和科學觀察以及估計，而 eTimeline of Revelation >"啟示-電子時間線"是基於聖經啟示錄中的時間尺度，但是開始時間非凡人所知。

Biblical, Chinese & World History Timeline - Users Guide

eTimeline consists of three timeline periods: 1. eTimeline of Creation - the distance past (from the creation of the heaven and the earth to the creation of Adam and Eve), 2. eTimeline of History - the immediate past (from the creation of Adam and Eve to the present), and 3. eTimeline of Revelation - the future (from the present to the new heaven and new earth):

The "distance past" is a period from the creation of the heaven and the earth as recorded in the Bible to the creation of Adam and Eve, and from the Big Bang as described by the scientific community to the appearance of human. The "immediate past" history includes notable characters and events of the Bible from Adam to Apostle Paul, and of the Chinese history from 3 Sovereigns & 5 Emperors to Tsai Ing-wen, Xi Jinping, and dynasties from Xia Dynasty to Republic of China (中華民國), People Republic of China (中华人民共和国), and events from taming of the great flood by Yu the Great (大禹治水) to the return of Hongkong and Macau to China (香港及澳門回歸中國). The "future" is a period from the present to the new heaven and new earth as recorded in the Book of Revelation.

To access the web site, open a browser, copy and paste the link below and place it in the URL space and press the Enter key. Link to eTimeline (https://jadelion02.github.io/eTimeline).

Opening Screen

When you first open the webpage, you will see an image similar to the one below. A drop-down menu with a transparent background appears on the top left area of the webpage. The menu can be closed with a click anywhere else on the screen outside this menu area, or click the X located on the top right corner of the menu. The menu can be activated anytime when the on the left side of the screen below the picture is pressed.

This menu is the top level of all webpages within this web site. Sub-menu that are available are indicated by a triangle next to each menu item, you can access the sub-menus by clicking the ▼ down arrow or to collapse it, click the ▲ up arrow.

Accessing the eTimeline

To access the timelines, choose the eTimeline in the Menu and make the selection.

You can either choose the link embedded in the name of the timeline at the center of the picture above the three circles . To rotate to a different timeline, click one of the circles or the left-right arrow on either side near the edge of the picture. The third method to access the timelines is to choose the embedded link in the name of the title of the timeline. Other than the menu, you can switch language to Chinese (中文 ♥) on this webpage and return to English right next to the main title of eTimeline.

eTimeline Navigation

If you are on a Timeline page, the navigation panel can be activated by clicking the （^ caret sign) button next to the "Home" button near the top of the screen. The navigation panel has most, if not all, the controls necessary for navigation. The control buttons on the navigation panel (shown on the right) control the horizontal movements of Timeline, four of these buttons are duplicated on the top right hand corner of the page. If you have a wheel mouse, two of the horizontal movements (left or right scroll) can be controlled by scrolling Up or Down using the mouse wheel, or by dragging the cursor left or right on the screen in the content area between the top and bottom time scales.

The " + " (plus sign) button zooms into the Timeline, whereas the " - " (minus sign) button zooms out. These zoom buttons allow you to either spread out the Timeline or compress the Timeline. If you find the search result items are too close together, press the + (plus sign) on the navigation panel or use the keyboard "Ctrl" key and mouse wheel Scroll Up function to expand the Timeline. If you wish to see more items on the screen, you can press the " - " (minus sign) or use the keyboard "Ctrl" key and mouse wheel Scroll Down function to compress the Timeline. Occasionally, when the items are too tightly packed together and the screen movement appears frozen, you may refresh the screen to restore the operation.

eTimeline Search

An item is the name of a person/character or an event in the database, and each item is associated with an identification number ID. To search for an item in the database, press the ▼ (down arrow) in the "Search ... ▾" box in the navigation panel. When the drop-down list appears, type in the search item in the white blank space below the search box. The auto-complete function will narrow down the search list while you type. Highlight and make your selection once you have located your item and press the "Submit" button . The item matching the search will move to the appropriate time frame and it will be highlighted in bold and coloured differently from the neighbouring items. Normally, it will appear near the centre of the screen, if you do not see the item, you may need to use the vertical slider to move up or down to locate the highlighted item.

On the left-hand side of the navigation panel, you will see a white rectangular box with a "1” in the box (i.e. ) next to the "Search ... ▾" box, this is the default search ID value of this webpage. Every search result will return an ID matching your search action. If you know or remember the ID number of the character or event, type the ID in the box and press the "Submit" button to jump to that point in time. For example, in the eTimeline of History, ID 1.01 will take you to Noah, and 1.02 will take you to Abraham. The check box next to the hour glass is to toggle between Activate/Deactivate the "Submit" button.

Hide and Unhide

Each Timeline screen is divided into horizontal groups sandwiched in between two identical time scales. Each group contains the relevant characters associated with the title heading located on the left-hand side of the screen, and each group can be made invisible by pressing the "Hide" button . Hiding a group does not delete the group, but just making it temporarily invisible from the viewer to facilitate reading. Pressing the general "Unhide" button located in the navigation panel will render all groups visible again.

Accessing the Bible Gateway and Blue Letter Bible

A link to the Bible Gateway and Blue Letter Bible web sites is provided for convenience only, these web sites are third party external web sites and have no association with eTimeline. To access the Bible Gateway or the Blue Letter Bible, open the navigation panel (see eTimeline Navigation), click the " + " (plus sign) located near the bottom left hand corner of the navigation panel. The drop-down panel will show the input boxes for their respective web sites. Simply type in your search subject in the input boxes and press the "Submit" button.

Miscellaneous Information

eTimeline of History is based on Hebrew Calendar starting at “1" (i.e. zero year, 1 is equivalent to 3,761 BC based on Hebrew Rabbinical tradition), however when you hover the mouse cursor over the point of interest, the years in Gregorian Calendar format will show up as a pop-up tool tip. The explanations of the commands are listed under the little green square with a question mark (i.e.). Just hover the mouse cursor over it and a drop-down menu will be shown. If you are using a touch screen, you can use pinch-to-zoom action on the screen (i.e. which is equivalent to the + and – functions on top of the screen of the navigation panel). Most of these functions are also available in the other timelines as well.

eTimeline of Creation is based on the time scale in the Book of Genesis and scientific observations as well as estimations, whereas eTimeline of Revelation is based on the time scale in the Book of Revelation with start time unknown to mortals.

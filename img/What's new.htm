What's new in eTimeline :

With the addition of the voice recognition function (Chrome & Edge Browsers only), spoken names can now be captured in the search bar of the "Names in the Bible" page when the microphone is activated.
[2023.06.12]

New families have been added to Genealogy.
[2023.05.27]

Genealogy has been updated in both HTM and PDF versions.
[2023.02.24]

A printable version of Genealogy in PDF format was added to make the web version of the same name more readable.
[2023.02.02]

A table contains many names in the Bible. This table can be accessed under Names in the menu. The names are seachable by using either English or Chinese in the search box. In addition, the Bible verses will appear when the cursor is hover over the particular verse. The verse in Chinese pop-up will appear behind the English pop-up, and can be accessed by mover the cursor to the Chinese pop-up without clicking the mouse.
[2022.10.15]

A search function in the Genealogy section and a link to the Bible Gateway & Blue Letter Bible have been added to facilitate the searching of person or persons by name in the genealogy of Jesus as well as in the table of nations according to chapter 10 of the Book of Genesis.
